Tips & Help

Keyboard: W (web), S (steam), R (roblox). Try the Konami code for a surprise.

How to use

Press the big buttons to open random links. Use Shuffle All to open one of each. You can favorite links, view history and use keyboard shortcuts.

Auto mode will open a random category at the interval you choose (in seconds).
